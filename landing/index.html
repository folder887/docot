docot is an opinionated, black-and-white messenger. Chat with people, plan your week, write wiki-style notes with backlinks, and follow a public news feed — without leaving the app.

What's inside

Real-time chats

Direct messages, groups, channels and bots. WebSocket delivery, end-to-end ready.

Calendar

Plan your week with a clean monthly grid and per-day agenda. No clutter.

Wiki notes

Markdown with [[wiki links]], backlinks, and a force-directed graph of your knowledge.

News feed

A simple, opinionated public timeline. Like, repost, reply.

Themes & wallpapers

Light, Dark, Paper, Inverse — with chat wallpapers in strict black-and-white.

Cross-platform

Web, Windows, macOS, Linux and Android. Native installers signed where possible.

Why black & white

Color is noise. docot strips messengers down to typography and rhythm — so chat history, your calendar, and your notes all look like one place. Less switching, less skimming, more reading.
